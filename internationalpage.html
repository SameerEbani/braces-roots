Frequently Asked Questions

Our customer support team is here to assist you. Start by browsing our FAQ section to find answers to your questions.

Do you offer painless dental treatments?

Yes! We use advanced tools like microscopes, digital scanners, and pain-free anesthesia techniques to ensure a comfortable, stress-free experience.

What technologies do you use during treatments?

We use CBCT, iTero scans, microscope RCTs, CAD/CAM crowns, and other advanced tools to deliver precision and speed.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our website or contact our clinic directly via phone or WhatsApp.

Is digital smile design available at your clinic?

Yes, our clinic offers digital smile design consultations using state-of-the-art imaging and planning software.

How long does a smile makeover take?

It depends on your treatment plan. Some patients may complete treatment in one visit, while others may take a few weeks.

Do you treat children and elderly patients?

Absolutely! Our clinic is family-friendly and equipped to handle dental needs across all age groups.

What safety measures do you follow post-COVID?

We maintain strict sterilization protocols, use PPE kits, and follow all health guidelines to ensure maximum safety for our patients and staff.

Do you provide EMI or flexible payment options?

Yes, we offer EMI options for eligible treatments. Please speak to our front desk for more details.

Still have questions?

Reach out, and we'll be happy to help.
