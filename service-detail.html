Why Smile Makeovers Aren’t
Just Vanity

Too many people hide their smiles due to discolored, chipped, or uneven teeth — affecting confidence, relationships, and how they show up in the world. A smile makeover isn’t about perfection. It’s about unlocking the version of you that feels most vibrant and free.

What’s Included in a
Braces & Roots Smile Makeover?

  • Customized plan — tailored to your face & goals
  • Digital Smile Simulation with iTero® Scanner
  • Veneers, bonding, whitening & gum contouring options
  • Minimally invasive techniques using lasers
  • Expert cosmetic dentists trained in facial aesthetics

Whether it’s one tooth or a full arch — our approach is subtle, tech-assisted, and always built around your face, not a template.
